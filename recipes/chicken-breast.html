Chicken Breast

Description

Need an easy way to fulfill your protein macros before the day ends? Try this easy recipe to satisfy your dietary needs with a large serving of protein within this white meat meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Black Pepper and Salt

Steps

  1. Defrost your simple chicken breast to prepare it as necessary
  2. Slice your chicken breast into nice servings
  3. Season both sides of your chicken breasts servings with black pepper and salt
  4. Place uncooked chicken breast onto heated non-stick pan, cook until both sides are seared golden
  5. Cut with a fork and knife to check if chicken has been thoroughly cooked
