Protein Shake

Description

Need an easy way to fulfill your protein macros before the day ends? Try this recipe for a protein filled drink that is perfect right after your workout

Ingredients

  • Blender Bottle
  • Gold Standard Whey Protein (Any Flavour) 1 scoop
  • Oat Milk 16oz

Steps

  1. Fill your bottle until the oatmilk fills up to the top of the blender ball
  2. Pour your serving size scoop of whey
  3. Fill in the rest of your oatmilk
  4. Give the bottle a real sturdy shake, thoroughly blend the drink
